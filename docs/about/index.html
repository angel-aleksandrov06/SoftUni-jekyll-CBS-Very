About the school

The main objective of CBS-Very is to provide quality tuition to students so that the joy of dance may be experienced. The teaching environment is warm, friendly and encouraging and many students have remained with the school from its outset to the present, some going on to full-time and tertiary dance courses. Classes aim to challenge students and the content is presented in an interesting format appropriate to the particular age and level. Children in "Minjons" level enjoy a mixture of dance games and basic technique while "Juniors" level are guided through a more formal class structure. Props and inspiring music form an important part of the learning process.

Our Mission

It is the Mission of CBS-Very "Classical Ballet School -Very" to provide quality, caring, enjoyable & achievable dance tuition to children in the local community. Ours is a holistic venture, nurturing the child in the art of dance by fostering confidence and imparting the values of kindness, compassion, fairness, group skills & positive work ethic. CBS-Very`s point of difference in the private dance school community is its strong adherence to the above philosophy & values. Striving always to put the child first is what makes our school attractive to modern families.

About the teacher

The leader of the school is Veriana Velichkova. Though she was born in Sofia, Bulgaria in 1970, first steps in Ballet had in the cultural center ‘’Iskra’’ at the town of Vidin.

In 1980 she was accepted a student at the National Ballet School of Bulgaria (now known as National School for Dancing Art).

After graduating in 1989 Veriana Velichkova began teaching in the cultural center at the town of Berkovitza. A few years later was working as a performing Ballerina at the Opera House in Varna city. From 2001 until 2010 was dancer and dance captain at Magic Life – an Austrian established Holiday chain, which is now part of TUI Germany. Her responsibility were the dancing shows at the club, the sport and recreation Program for the guests and she had the chance to put on stage dancing shows for children. There she developed her group training skills in the area of Aerobics, including Step, Tae Bo, Stretching, Pilates, Flexibar, BBP… - the company teaching the dancers at Magic Life in the way of sports was AFAS (Austrian Fitness and Aerobic School).

2016 she graduated as a yoga teacher at the International Certified Yoga School in Isny, Germany and in 2017 Pilates Instructor.
