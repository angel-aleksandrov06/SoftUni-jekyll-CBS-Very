About the school

The main objective of CBS-Very is to provide quality tuition to students so that the joy of dance may be experienced. The teaching environment is warm, friendly and encouraging and many students have remained with the school from its outset to the present, some going on to full-time and tertiary dance courses. Classes aim to challenge students and the content is presented in an interesting format appropriate to the particular age and level. Children in "Kinds" level enjoy a mixture of dance games and basic technique while "Juniors" level are guided through a more formal class structure. Props and inspiring music form an important part of the learning process.

Our Mission

It is the Mission of CBS-Very "Classical Ballet School -Very" to provide quality, caring, enjoyable & achievable dance tuition to children in the local community. Ours is a holistic venture, nurturing the child in the art of dance by fostering confidence and imparting the values of kindness, compassion, fairness, group skills & positive work ethic. CBS-Very`s point of difference in the private dance school community is its strong adherence to the above philosophy & values. Striving always to put the child first is what makes our school attractive to modern families.
